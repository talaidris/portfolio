Welcome to McMaster Ismaili Student Association!

What is Our Goal?

Our mission is to connect Ismaili students at McMaster University, create a supportive community, and promote both personal and collective growth through mentorship, sports, and wellness initiatives.

Welcome to MISA — where campus life meets community! 🌟
